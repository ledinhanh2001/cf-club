Better Coffee for a Better World

Looking for a fun, unique, sneakily educational outing? Join us for a monthly Public Coffee Tasting or “Cupping” (think wine tasting without the alcohol). From coffee novice to budding expert, coffee cuppings will foster a new sense of appreciation for your everyday brew. Participants will sample an array of high-end coffees while getting a behind the scenes, industry expert rundown of each sample. Cuppings are held at The Lab (Private Coffee Space on Floor Two of the Paper Mill Building on The Historic Landing) once a month, and are $25/person. Each participant will leave with a bag of their favorite sample from the cupping and a wealth of new industry insight.
